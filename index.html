Who We Are!

We envision a world where healthcare is accessible, affordable, and excellent

Cangenesis Healthcare is a premier medical tourism company dedicated to providing world-class healthcare services to patients from around the globe. Our mission is to facilitate access to high-quality medical treatments, combining top-notch medical expertise with personalized care in some of the world's leading healthcare destinations.

Treatments We Offer

We Aim to Improve Health Outcomes and Enhance the Quality of Life For Our Patients

Oncology

Our team of highly experienced oncologists, surgeons, and healthcare professionals work collaboratively.

Hematology

We offer Hematology with group of medicine that focuses on the study, diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of diseases related to blood.

Radiation Treatment

Radiation treatment, also known as radiotherapy, is a medical use of ionizing radiation to treat cancer and other conditions by damaging the DNA of targeted cells.

Cardiac, Pediatric Cardiac

Cardiac Department is renowned for its excellence in providing advanced cardiac care. Our services cover a wide spectrum of heart conditions.

Transplants

Liver and Kidney Transplant Department is recognized for its high success rates in our treatment program.

Orthopedic & Spine

Orthopedic & Spine Department is dedicated to diagnosing, treating, and rehabilitating musculoskeletal conditions.

Neurology

The Neurology Department offers specialized care for a wide range of neurological conditions. Our team of highly skilled neurologists

Nephrology and Urology

Nephrology and Urology is the field of medicine that focuses on diagnosing and treating diseases that affect the kidneys.

Gastrology

Gastrology, often referred to as gastroenterology, is the branch of medicine focused on the digestive system and its disorders.
